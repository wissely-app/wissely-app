1. What this covers

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") describes Wissely's uptime target for the API and dashboard, our support response times, and what happens if we miss them. It applies to paid subscriptions (Starter, Growth, Pro); the free trial is provided as-is, without an uptime commitment.

2. Uptime commitment

We target 99.9% monthly uptime for the Wissely API and dashboard, measured as the percentage of time these are available and responding to requests, excluding the exclusions in Section 5.

Monthly uptimeService credit
Below 99.9%10% of that month's subscription fee
Below 99.0%25% of that month's subscription fee
Below 95.0%50% of that month's subscription fee

Credits are applied to a future invoice and must be requested within 30 days of the incident via a support ticket. Current and historical status is published at status.wissely.com.

3. Support response times

These are enforced automatically by our support system, not a manual best-effort target:

  • Pro plan: first response within 4 business hours.
  • Starter and Growth plans: first response within 1-2 business days.

Business hours are Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm in our support team's local time zone, excluding public holidays.

4. What counts as downtime

Downtime means the Wissely API or dashboard returning error responses to a majority of requests, or being completely unreachable, for more than 5 consecutive minutes. A single failed request, or intermittent errors affecting a small fraction of traffic, do not count as downtime on their own.

5. Exclusions

The uptime commitment does not cover:

  • Scheduled maintenance, announced at least 24 hours in advance via the status page.
  • Outages caused by our upstream AI provider (Anthropic) or other third-party infrastructure we depend on and don't control.
  • Issues caused by the customer's own network, browser, or misuse of the API (e.g. exceeding rate limits).
  • Force majeure events - anything genuinely outside our reasonable control.

6. Changes to this SLA

We may update this SLA from time to time. Material changes that reduce the commitment will be announced at least 30 days in advance to active subscribers.

7. Contact

Questions about this SLA, or to request a service credit, go through Support.
